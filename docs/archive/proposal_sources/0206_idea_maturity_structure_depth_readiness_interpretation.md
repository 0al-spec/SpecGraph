# 0206 Idea Maturity Structure Depth Readiness Interpretation

Source draft for proposal `0206`.

`candidate_structure_depth` should remain objective Metrics telemetry. SpecGraph
should interpret shallow structural observations in its own
`readiness_explainers` surface so SpecSpace can tell the operator why a product
candidate narrative still feels shallow without treating the raw counts as a
Metrics-defined score or promotion gate.
